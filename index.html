About Me

Hi, I'm Sadman Sakib Alif, currently serving as an Adjunct Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BRAC University, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

My research focuses on Machine Learning for healthcare, with a particular emphasis on medical image analysis and multimodal learning. I am also passionate about Physical AI, working on integrating intelligent models into real-world systems.
